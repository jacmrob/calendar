Introducing the Concentric Calendar

An intuitive way to keep track of time. 

$ cd your_repo_root/repo_name
$ git fetch origin
$ git checkout gh-pages

How do I read it?

Each circle represents a unit of time, with a radius that grows dynamically in response to larger units. Starting from the center, minutes (yellow) grow until they fill an hour, hours (blue) grow until they fill a day, days (purple) grow until they fill a month, and months (grey) grow until they fill a year (white). When all circles are full, the year is up!

Updates in real-time

Using Javascript's world clock, will be 100% accurate whenever you refresh your page.
